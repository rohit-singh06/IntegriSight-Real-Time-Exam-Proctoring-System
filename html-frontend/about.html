THE PROJECT

What is IntegriSight?

IntegriSight is an AI-powered, real-time exam proctoring system built to uphold academic integrity in online assessments. As digital education grows, so does the need for reliable, automated exam supervision.

Using Vanilla JS, Flask, MediaPipe, and OpenCV, IntegriSight monitors students through their webcam — detecting suspicious behaviors like gaze deviation, multiple faces, and face absence — all automatically, without requiring a human proctor.

Proctors can create AI-generated MCQ tests, assign them to specific students, and monitor live sessions with real-time risk scoring.
